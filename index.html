Pizza Trivia

Who found about pizza first and when did it happen?

Pizza, as we know it today, originated in Naples, Italy, in the late 18th to early 19th century. Poor working-class Neapolitans began adding tomatoes, introduced to Europe from the Americas, to flatbreads. This innovation led to the modern pizza. The earliest written record of pizza was in 997 AD in Gaeta, Italy, referring to a flatbread. However, its global recognition came in 1889 when Queen Margherita of Savoy tried a pizza resembling the Italian flag's colors (tomatoes, mozzarella, basil), giving rise to the famous "Margherita pizza."
